About us

Today, many people prefer to celebrate family holidays, birthdays, and weddings in the Dubno restaurant and the Smyga banquet hall.

And once the catering establishments were not at all like the presentable, fashionable Golden Lion restaurant, created in order to have a good rest, tasty food, and have a good time.

The history of the restaurant business goes back to ancient times. In ancient Rome, thermopoly is the prototype of the present restaurant Dubno, food and hot wines were served. However, those establishments were more reminiscent of today's express cafes, because there were no seats there.

In the 7th century in China during the great Song dynasty, specialized restaurants appeared - visitors could treat themselves to only one particular dish. These establishments are already more reminiscent of today's restaurants in Kremenets in terms of services provided.

But visitors in a roadside tavern or inn in the Middle Ages sometimes had to be content with being served, let's say - a complex lunch, often in terrible unsanitary conditions. It was only in the 18th century that restaurants with exquisite menus, luxurious interiors and chic furnishings appeared in France and Spain. Restourabo (restoration from French) - these were the names of these establishments.

Further, the restaurant business developed at a rapid pace towards creating comfort for customers. The menu expanded, it was possible to order a dish to your taste, separate booths and tables appeared.
